About Team

  • Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a famous team in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
  • The team represents the city of Bengaluru, Karnataka.
  • RCB plays its home matches at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium.
  • The team has had star players like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Chris Gayle.
  • RCB is loved by millions of cricket fans around the world.

Latest RCB News

  • RCB won their first IPL title in 2025 after defeating Punjab Kings in the final.
  • The IPL 2026 season will start on March 28 with RCB playing against Sunrisers Hyderabad.
  • RCB will play several home matches at M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru.
